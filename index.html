Privacy warning

Only scan receipts and invoices. Do not scan documents containing Social Security Numbers or government ID numbers. The tool automatically redacts sensitive patterns but is designed for financial receipts, not identity documents.

Scanning receipts

Open Scan Receipt. Choose the document type. Tap Open Camera. Your camera opens immediately. Take the photo. The app reads it and speaks what it found. Add a note or tap Dictate Note to speak a note. Tap Add to Audit or Flag as Suspicious.

Missing receipts

Scroll down in the Scan section. Fill in what you know and tap Log Missing Receipt. Absence of documentation is itself evidence.

Running the audit

Open Run Audit. Tap Run Forensic Audit. Tap Quick Audio Summary for a 30-second spoken briefing. Tap Find the Smoking Gun for full AI analysis read aloud.

What the flags mean

DUPLICATE — same vendor paid same amount twice.
NEPOTISM — approver name matches vendor name.
VELOCITY — multiple payments same vendor same day.
MONOPOLY — one vendor gets most of a department budget.
BENFORD — numbers do not follow natural math. Possible fabrication.
MANUAL FLAG — you marked this suspicious yourself.
MISSING — receipt requested but not produced.
