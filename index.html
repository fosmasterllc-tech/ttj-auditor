Scanning receipts

Open Scan Receipt. Tap Open Camera or Upload Image. The app reads the document and speaks what it found. Add a note by typing or tapping Dictate Note and speaking. Tap Add to Audit to save, or Flag as Suspicious to mark it high-priority.

Missing receipts

If they refuse to produce a receipt, scroll down in the Scan section. Fill in what you know and tap Log Missing Receipt. Absence of documentation is itself evidence of non-compliance.

Running the audit

Open Run Audit. Tap Run Forensic Audit. Tap Quick Audio Summary for a 30-second briefing. Tap Find the Smoking Gun for full AI analysis read aloud.

Building evidence

Open Build Evidence Package. Tap Build. Everything is compiled into a numbered legal document you can print or export and send to an attorney or journalist.

What the flags mean

DUPLICATE — same vendor paid same amount twice.
NEPOTISM — approver name matches vendor name.
VELOCITY — multiple payments same vendor same day.
MONOPOLY — one vendor gets most of a department budget.
BENFORD — numbers do not follow natural math. Possible fabrication.
MANUAL FLAG — you marked this suspicious yourself.
MISSING — receipt requested but not produced.
